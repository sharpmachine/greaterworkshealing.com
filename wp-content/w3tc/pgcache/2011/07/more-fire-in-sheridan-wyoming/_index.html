More Fire in Sheridan Wyoming

Posted on July 26, 2011 by Mark Kuntz

Wow!! our meetings in Sheridan continued with His Holy Fire falling on most who attended. I am amazed at Daddy’s goodness and love for His children. Once again people were healed as they sat in their seats. Many were filled with the Holy Spirit and Holy laughter broke out after one meeting.

The pastor gave birth to spiritual twins as he recieved a word about birthing in not one, but two babies. Some recieved a major deliverance and many were emotionally healed as His love fell. The Lord challenged us to get off the milk, and learn to eat His meat, to walk in His fullness as mature sons and daughters of God, into the abundant life of walking in the Spirit.
